About EBMO LTD

EBMO LTD is a multi-service company headquartered in Hounslow, Greater London. We specialize in delivering high-quality services across event catering, private security, and professional cleaning. Our vision is to redefine standards and exceed expectations by combining skill, innovation, and customer-centric care.

We serve clients ranging from corporate institutions and residential estates to event organizers and individual households. Every project, big or small, is treated with personalized attention and a commitment to excellence.
